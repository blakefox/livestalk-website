Designed for Ranchers by Ranchers!

As a Ranchers son, turned software engineer, watching all the advances in technology in the past 20 years, I have been mystified that little of these technologies have made their way to the Agriculture Sector. What does the current method of Livestock Management even look like? For most operations, its just comments written in a notebook or notes on the back of a hand, with hopes of formally documenting everything back at the main site.

Livestalk aims to change that! We used Computer Vision paired with an Intuitive User Experience to Simplify and automate the time consuming elements of Livestock Record Keeping. Our mission is to

  • Simplify the Livestock Record Creation and Retrieval Process

  • Transferable and Shareable to others when needed.

Our Approach

In order to provide the best Livestock Management Solution, we needed to analyze what is lacking in the current solutions available on the Market.

Encorage Accuracy

Current Technologies do not create any incentive to record keep. How can we provide incentive to keep accurate records on livestock?

Simplify Technology

Technology is hard and ever changing. How can we harness the use of technology in a way that is intuitive for the user?

Sharing Data

Livestock Managers make thier living via raising and selling Livestock. On transfer, why is it common to not share past records on the individual livestock?

Product Features
